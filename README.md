# MoneySaver

**MoneySaver** is a modern and intuitive personal finance manager designed to help you track expenses, manage budgets, and improve your financial habits effortlessly.

Whether you want to monitor daily spending, organize your savings, or maintain full control over your finances, MoneySaver provides almost everything you need in one simple and elegant application.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📊 **Expense & Income Tracking**  
  Easily record and categorize your transactions.

- 💰 **Budget Management**  
  Set budgets and keep your spending under control.

- 📈 **Statistics & Charts**  
  Visualize your financial habits with detailed analytics.

- 🔒 **Secure Local Data Storage**  
  Your data stays stored locally for better privacy and security.

- ⚡ **Fast & User-Friendly Interface**  
  Smooth experience designed for everyday use.

- 🌙 **Modern & Clean UI**  
  Elegant design with a focus on usability.

---

## 🚀 Why MoneySaver?

Managing money should be simple.

MoneySaver helps you:

- Understand where your money goes
- Save more efficiently
- Plan future expenses
- Build healthier financial habits

---

## 🖥️ Technologies

Built using modern web technologies:

- **MUI (Material UI)**

Optimized for performance, simplicity, and responsiveness.
